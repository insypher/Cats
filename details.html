Job Summary

On behalf of Zanlink Limited, we are looking to hire an Accounts Receivable and Revenue Assurance Accountant. His/her main roles will be Monitoring and reconciling all incoming receivables, Monitoring customers approved credits and Reconciliation of customers' accounts.

Location: Zanzibar

Duties And Responsibilities

  • Preparing monthly billing trial run and explain variance from a prior run.

  • Generate bill run and auto dispatch to customers beginning each month. Ensure all customers have received invoices by the 3rd of each month and share the final dispatch report to Management.

  • Perform daily and weekly revenue reconciliations for all customers including new sales, upgrades, downgrades, suspensions, and churn to ensure appropriate billing and revenue assurance.

  • Make daily follow up on debtors via emails, calls, and physical visits. Ensure our debt collection SoPs for follow-up are implemented and followed to realise maximum collection and meet our collection targets. Ensure overdue debt is given higher priority for collection

  • Through weekly meetings with HoF and MD, suggest actions such as ceasing accounts of difficult debtors, payment plans, involving 3rd party debt collector, or other appropriate actions to ensure debt is collected.

  • Ensure all credit notes raised in the subsequent month for previous billings have valid documentation and obtain approval from the MD to post them.

  • Maintain accurate debtors always aging reports with updated customer status.

  • Prepare documentation for debt write-off and monthly update of provision for bad debts-ECL through IFRS 9 schedule.

  • Prepare, weekly and monthly, collection reports, revenue reconciliation schedules, Legal reports, MRR reports (New sales, upgrades, suspension, downgrades, and churn), monthly deferred revenue schedules, Active circuits reconciliation, and revenue commentaries V/S previous month.

  • Perform weekly reconciliation between the billings and the active circuits
